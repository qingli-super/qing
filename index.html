Job Market Paper · Manuscript under preparation

Adoption and Learning in Enterprise Knowledge Systems

This project studies adoption and learning in enterprise knowledge systems. Through staged access and follow-up outreach, it examines Knowledge Base (KB), which converts raw interactions, documents, and conversations into usable knowledge, and Knowledge Intelligence (KI), which converts top-performing employees’ behaviors into reusable and replicable success.

Working Papers

Major revision · Management Science

Artificial Intelligence as a Relational Shock: Evidence on Newcomer–Seasoned Collaboration in Sales Organizations

with Jialie Chen and Danny Wang

AI adoption increases seasoned salespeople’s workload and crowds out learning opportunity for newcomers, revealing a hidden organizational cost of workflow-embedded AI.

Invited to resubmit · Marketing Science

AI–Human Collaboration in Sequential Sales Funnels: Evidence from a Large-Scale Field Experiment

with Xiaolin Li, Danny Wang, and Flora Gu

In a field experiment with 78,169 prospects, AI-initiated outreach provide more accurate information, increase agent's trust, makes later human follow-up more productive and nearly doubles loan-application odds.

Manuscript under preparation

Work Hard or Work Smart? Layered Generative AI and Salesperson Effort Allocation in Customer-Acquisition Operations

with Yijing Li, Albert Liu, Danny Wang, and Flora Gu

A field experiment shows that summary tools broaden lead quantity, while diagnostic tools shift salesperson effort toward engagement quality. We interpret these patterns through Job Demands–Resources theory and sales agents’ cognitive and metacognitive processes.

Manuscript under preparation

Visual, Vocal, or Both? Decomposing the Value of Human Presence in AI-Generated Video Advertising

A 2 × 2 field experiment shows that visual and vocal cues can have different effects on engagement, conversion, and applicant quality.

Research in progress

When AI Crowds Out the Human Touch: A Randomized Field Experiment in Social Commerce Sales

with Jialie Chen and Danny Wang

This project studies when AI-driven outreach complements versus displaces the relational selling that drives conversion in social commerce.
